History of Chess

The game of Chess has been around for more than 1500 years. The origins are still unknown, However, the earliest players can be traced back to 7th century India. From there it was introduced to Persia. In Persia, chess was primarily played by nobility. Persia contributed several factors to the game, including the phrases "Shah"(Persian for "King") and "Shah Mat"( Persian for "the King is helpless" or checkmate). After Persia was conquered during an islamic conquest, Chess was introduced to the Muslim world. From There Chess spread throughout Europe and eventually the entire globe.

Chess pieces

  • Rook ♖: The Rook can move any amount of space both vertically and horizontally.
  • Knight ♘: The Knight can move in an L shape(1 space to the right/left then 2 spaces down/up)
  • Bishop ♗: The Bishop can move diagonally on the board in any direction.
  • Queen ♕: The Queen, the most versatile of the pieces, can move vertically, horizontally diagonally in any direction.
  • King ♔: The king can move one space in any direction, the objective of the game is to capture the opponents king.
  • Pawn ♙: The pawn is capable of moving one space forward, unless its the pawns first move, then it can move two spaces forward.
